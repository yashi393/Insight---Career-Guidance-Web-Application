Computer Vision & Graphics

Computer Vision (CV) and Graphics combine algorithms, deep learning, and rendering techniques to interpret, generate and manipulate visual information. Applications span autonomous vehicles, medical imaging, AR/VR, film & VFX, 3D reconstruction, and generative imaging. Recent advances in vision transformers, NeRFs and generative models have accelerated demand for engineers who bridge research and production.

Pros & Cons

Pros

• Extremely high demand across multiple industries (automotive, healthcare, entertainment)

• Strong salary growth for model / production experience

• Research & creative opportunities (papers, VFX, AR/VR products)

• Cross-discipline roles: hardware, vision, graphics and UX

Cons

• Heavy math, research and GPU resource requirements

• Often requires expensive hardware (training / inference)

• Fast-moving field — continuous learning required

• Productionizing models has additional engineering complexity
