Cloud Computing

Cloud Computing powers modern tech infrastructure by providing compute, storage, networking, security, and DevOps automation over the internet. Organizations use the cloud to scale applications, reduce costs, deploy global services, and adopt architectures like microservices, containers, and serverless computing. Cloud is one of the fastest-growing tech domains due to the rise of AI, digital transformation, and remote-first businesses.
