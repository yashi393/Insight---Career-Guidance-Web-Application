Data Science and Analytics

Data Science & Analytics (DSA) involves extracting knowledge and insights from data using statistical methods, programming, and domain expertise. It helps organizations make data-driven decisions, forecast trends, build dashboards, and derive actionable intelligence from raw data. With rising data-driven business culture, DSA powers business intelligence, reporting, predictive analytics, and supports AI/ML workflows.
