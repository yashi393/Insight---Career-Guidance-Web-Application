Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML) focuses on building systems that can learn patterns, make decisions, and improve performance without explicit programming. It involves understanding algorithms, data processing, model training, and evaluating intelligent behavior. AIML powers recommendation systems, chatbots, autonomous vehicles, and predictive analytics, blending mathematics, programming, and problem-solving to create real-world intelligent solutions.
